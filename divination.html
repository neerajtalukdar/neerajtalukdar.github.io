Divination: Interactive LSTM Text Generation using p5.js

This uses a pre-trained model on the corpus of Harry Potter books



Use the sliders to adjust the length and accuracy of the prediction. Length determines the number of characters in the resulting predicted text. The longer the length, more time is required for computation. The accuracy slider affects the randomness of the output, 0 being highly random where result might not vene be proper English , while accuracy of 1 will be highly representative of text from the books.
