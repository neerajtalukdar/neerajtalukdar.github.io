About Me

Namaste! I am Neeraj, a UX Designer currently working at Cisco, Boston. I hail from Guwahati, a small city nestled among the pristine hills in north-eastern India. I completed my undergrad in Design from IIT Guwahati after which I decided to pursue my Masters in HCI from Carnegie Mellon University. My interests and specializations span across a wide variety of topics such as Augmented Reality, Machine Learning, Tangible Interfaces and Conversational UIs. I consider myself a design technologist, with a passion for creating novel experiences in the intersection of art and technology. In my spare time, you will find me sketching, building odd gadgets, listening to Pink Floyd or playing football.
