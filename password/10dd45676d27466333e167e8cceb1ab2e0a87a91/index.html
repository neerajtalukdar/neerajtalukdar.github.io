Agent X

How can we leverage AI to augment agents in their everyday tasks and help them become Super Agents?
  • Categories: Competitor Analysis,Prototyping
  • Timeline: Nov 2017 - present
  • Team Members: Rajiv Krishnan (Product Manager), Gang Ding (Data Scientist/Developer), Harun Gadatia (Data Scientist/Developer)
  • Mentor: Prudvi Raj Dommaraju
What is this Project About?

This section of the project primarily focuses on the ideation and concpetualization of some of the next generation of agent interfaces and the role that AI can play in creating "Super Agents", essentially AI augmenting agents' capabilities

What was my contribution?

This was mostly a solo endeavour at understanding the AI landscape and coming up with prospective solutions and prototypes to envision the future of agent experiences

Overview

The prototypes and ideas covered in this section highlight some of the ways in which AI can be leveraged in the domain of contact center especially pertaining to agent experiences. Some of these ideas were explored as part of hackathons in creating proof of concepts

Alexa Integration

This idea was focused on the concept of being able to trigger voice calls to contact centers through Alexa

Voice Activated Headsets

This concept focuses on Cisco headsets and how they can be leveraged for a voice activated agent desktop

Smart Notes

This idea highlights the capability of a feature called Smart Notes which agents can leverage for note taking capabilities
