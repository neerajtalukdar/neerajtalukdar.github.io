Sportify

In this project, I worked on a conceptual app for enabling sports fans to stay connected with their favorite teams and clubs.

Problem Statement

There are several resources, apps and services which cater to sports fans across the globe. One has to go to Twitter, Reddit, Facebook to stay up to date and engage in news and discussions. There are separate Message Groups that also help bring together fans of a specfic club but these are also isolated.

Solution

Sportify is an app that provides a way to combine the fan experience for your favorite club in one single place - News feed, player updates, fan discussions etc.

Project Info

Time: 1 day

Theme: Mobile App, Sports

Contribution: This was a self-initiated design challenge.

A TLDR version of the project

This is a design challenge done over a weekend

I conducted a quick research session with a couple of friends who are big sports fans to gather inputs on sports consumption behavior

I did 1 round of evaluation on wireframes and refined them to arrive at the final designs

"Some people think football is a matter of life and death. I don't like that attitude. I can assure them it is much more serious than that." - Bill Shankly

This quote gives an accurate description of what sports means to a lot of people. Being a big sports fan myself, I wanted to take up a design challenge with the theme of sports. I found a design challenge online which went: Design an app for fans which helps follow their favorite team. In the next few sections, I have documented my process as I went about doing this design challenge.

Problem definition and re-framing

As an initial step, I did an ecosystem dump of all the thoughts that were streaming through my mind when I started analyzing the problem statement. The objective was to get to a point where I could define the scope of the problem much better. On further evaluation, I realized that a good problem to tackle would be to have a single place for sports fans, specifically for ones who support teams or clubs to have a single stop to get information regarding their team of all kinds - stories, news, information about games, upcoming matches, stats about games and palyers etc.

Create an app for Sports fans which would act as the single stop for them follow all relevant information about their team

Understanding the goals and needs

I set up some time with 3 of my friends of mine who I know are big sports enthususiasts. The objective of the research was to answer the following questions?

1. What are the different apps and services they use to keep up to date about news about their teams?
2. What are some pain-points and opportunity areas in these apps and services?
3. What is their experience like while following a game - lead-up, during and post-game?

Here is a summary of some of the interesting findings from the research sessions

1. There are several apps out there which helps people stay up to date with sports information, and at a high level it can be classified into three different categories
Social media apps the most notable ones being Twitter, Facebook pages, Reddit threads for interacting with other fans
Sports specific apps like Cricnews, One Football etc. for live scores and updates
News/Notification based services in general like Google Cards for news and updates

2. These apps are used for gathering news, following scores and updates, interacting with other fans, sharing joy/frustration, researching on players

3. A lot of information sharing happens over messaging apps as well, sharing of links to twitter and facebook posts, news articles and also direct opinion or info sharing through messages. It was common to find that WhatsApp groups would exists specifically to discuss sports especially catering to a particular team.

4. While following the game they would actively want to keep track of news about their team - injuries, potential line ups etc. During the game they would want to watch it with their friends and family either live at someone's home for bigger occasions or go to a sports bar to share the experience with other fans. At the same time, they would want to be in close communication with their sports fan groups through messaging apps. They would go on to social media apps to see what others are talking about after the game is done.

Brainstorming on high-level features

Based on the findings, I tried to define the high level features of the app which I wanted to keep in mid while going about creating the IA and wireframes

Consolidation

Ability to consolidate news and posts from multiple resources

Group Messaging

It should provide fans to create sub groups for discussions

Live scores

Basic feature to keep track of live scores and updates

News and updates

Collate fans relevant news articles about their team

Social Media

Provide fans a platform to enagage wiyth fans all over the world

Stats

Analyze stats and trivia on player's and teams' performance





High-Level Wireframing

I put together some high-level wireframes for the purpose of giving a tangible shape to the feature ideation. The objective was to eventually do an evaluation with my intial participants.





Some feedback and iteration
Table view not required

Upcoming matches need not be a table as one team will obviously always be the team you support. Also, one would mostly be interested in only the next 2-3 games at max







Details about articles

The source of the article and reading time would be critical for users to decide whether to read an article or not.







Persistent scores

The scores should be available from all sections of the app during a live game. This would allow users to keep a tab on the scores while also engaging in social media posts or chatting with their friends and other fans.
