What is this Project About?

Very often, we are faced with a situation where we want to keep tab on a game but canʼt do it actively due to various constraints, for eg. in office, where one cannot simply watch a game as one is expected to work. It becomes difficult to follow scores and one needs to often look at the score on the phone or in a separate tab, with the risk of getting reprimanded.

What is Sports Buddy?

SportsBuddy is a peripheral display robot for keeping tab on sports events of your interest. We take the example of soccer to highlight some of the that could be possible with SportsBuddy.
