Dignify

An app for dementia caretakers
  • Categories: Research,UI Design, Usability Testing
  • Timeline: Oct 2016
  • Team Members: Tony Deng
  • Client: Mark Stone
What is this Project About?

Dementia is a general term for decline in mental ability severe enough to interfere with daily life. Alzheimer’s is one of the most common forms of dementia. 1 in 9 people aged 65 and older have Alzheimer’s and it will cost the U.S. an estimated $236 billion in 2016. It’s the 5th leading cause of death among those 65 and older and 15 million caregivers gave an estimated 18.1 billion hours of unpaid care in 2015. Dignify is an interconnected system which aims to alleviate the financial and emotional burden experienced by families caring for a loved one in the early stages of dementia.
